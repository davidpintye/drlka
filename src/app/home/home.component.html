Ecologist, studying the dispersal interactions mediated by waterbirds. I am interested in a wide range of organisms dispersed by waterbirds, ranging from vascular plants to zooplankton (especially Bryozoans and Porifera). I also study the dispersal of antibiotic-resistant bacteria. My work contributes to the complex interactions in wetlands with the aim of understanding how climate change and human impacts affect these important waterbird-mediated ecosystem services.
